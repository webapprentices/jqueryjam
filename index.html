Welcome

Narrowing the skills gap one chapter at a time.

jQuery jAM is an initiative coordinated through Boston PHP Meetup, a not-for-profit organization focused on technology education. The initiative began life in September 2011 as a virtual self-study group designed to help members learn new technology skills.

jQuery jAM, Season 5 begins on Sunday, February 8, 2015. This season will provide intermediate to advanced training in how to develop a Single Page Web Application (SPA). The textbook we will use is Single Page Web Applications: JavaScript end-to-end.

This website is being developed as a value-added resource for Web designers and developers interested in achieving proficiency in JavaScript, jQuery and jQuery Mobile technologies.

To stay informed simply become a member of Boston PHP Meetup. The group is free to join and the training is virtual, so you don't need to live in the Boston area to participate. Our Twitter and Facebook feeds will also keep you posted on upcoming virtual training activities.
